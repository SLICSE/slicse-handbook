# Nutika õpitaristu käsiraamat informaatikaõpetajale
Nutika õpitaristu käsiraamat koondab teoreetilised alused, õppekavaarenduse, õppematerjalid, metoodikad, hindamisvahendid ning tehnoloogilised lahendused, mis toetavad digiloovtööde ja projektipõhise informaatikaõppe rakendamist Eesti koolides.

Käsiraamat põhineb doktoritööl „A Reference Framework for Smart Learning Infrastructure in Computer Science Education“ ning kirjeldab nutika õpitaristu põhikomponente, nende seoseid ja rakendamist digiloovtöö kontekstis.

Digiloovtööd käsitletakse käsiraamatus nutika õpitaristu keskse rakendamisena, mille kaudu toetatakse koostööd, probleemilahendust, disainmõtlemist, agiilseid tööviise ja varast inseneeriaharidust.

# Smart Learning Infrastructure Handbook for Computer Science Teachers

Smart Learning Infrastructure Handbook brings together the theoretical foundations, curriculum development, learning resources, pedagogical approaches, assessment tools, and technological solutions
that support the implementation of digital prototyping projects and project-based computer science education in Estonian schools.

The handbook is based on the doctoral dissertation “A Reference Framework for Smart Learning Infrastructure in Computer Science Education” and describes the core components of a smart learning infrastructure, their interrelationships, and their implementation within digital prototyping projects.

Within the handbook, digital prototyping projects are presented as the central use case for a smart learning infrastructure, through which collaboration, problem-solving, design thinking, agile ways of Working, and early engineering education are supported.
